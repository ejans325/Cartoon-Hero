Scooby-Doo

About Scooby

Scooby is a Great Dane with some human characteristics. He speaks with a distinct speech impediment, loves to eat, and is afraid of most things. His best friend is Shaggy Rogers, who also has an endless love for food and is easily frightened. The infamously hungry duo can be tricked into luring out the bad guys for a few Scooby Snacks.

Fun Facts

  • Scooby-Doo was named after a line in Frank Sinatra’s song “Strangers in the Night” where Frank sings “doo-be-doo-be-doo.”
  • He evolved from a bongo-playing Sheepdog/Great Dane mix named Too Much.
  • Scooby-Doo has been dubbed in at least 19 different languages.
